REFUND POLICY

Kero Tech Logistics' refund policy ensures that riders are fairly compensated if their trip doesn't meet expectations. Here are some key points:

When You Might Get a Refund:

  • Fare or Fee Charged in Error: If you were charged incorrectly, you may be eligible for a refund.
  • Significant Fare Difference: If the trip price was significantly higher than the estimate shown in the app, and it wasn't due to added stops or a changed destination, you might get a refund.
  • Trip Not Completed: If your trip was not completed within a reasonable walking distance to your requested destination, you could be eligible for a refund.
  • Trip Not Taken: If you or the intended rider did not take the trip, or there are other fraud concerns, a refund may be issued.

When You Might Not Get a Refund:

  • Vehicle Quality: Issues related to the vehicle quality or type of vehicle dispatched are generally not eligible for refunds.
  • Driver Quality: Problems with the driver not meeting quality expectations should be addressed through Kero Tech Logisticsâ€™ 2-way rating system or customer support, rather than a refund.
  • User Error: Issues caused by user error, such as requesting the wrong type of vehicle, are not eligible for refunds.
  • Uncontrollable Events: Delays or routing issues due to uncontrollable events like traffic or construction are not eligible for refunds.

How to Request a Refund:

  • Report the Issue: Report any issues within 7 days of the trip.
  • Kero Tech Logistics' Discretion: All refund requests are evaluated at Kero Tech Logistics' sole discretion and on a case-by-case basis.
